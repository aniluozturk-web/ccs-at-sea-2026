Practical Information

Venue & Travel

Directions and general information for conference participants

Conference Venue

The conference will be held at Juridicum, the Faculty of Law at Lund University. The building is located in the centre of Lund, between the Central Station and the Cathedral, and is approximately a five-minute walk from Lund C.

Address Lilla Gråbrödersgatan 4, 222 22 Lund
Building Juridicum, Lund University

Travelling to Lund

The nearest international airport is Copenhagen Airport (CPH) in Denmark. Lund is connected to the airport by direct train across the Øresund Bridge. The total journey from the airport to the conference venue takes approximately one hour.

Copenhagen Airport (CPH) → Lund Central

Øresundståg ~50 min Every 20–30 min

The Øresundståg (Øresund regional train) operates direct services from Copenhagen Airport to Lund Central Station. The train station is located directly beneath Terminal 3 — follow the signs marked "Trains" / "Tog" from the arrivals hall.

Tickets may be purchased via the Skånetrafiken app, at ticket machines in the station, or through the Öresundståg website.

Border control: Swedish identity checks may be conducted at Hyllie station (the first stop in Sweden). A valid passport or national identity card is required for the crossing.

Malmö Central → Lund Central

Pågatåg / Øresundståg ~12 min 4–6 trains per hour

Participants arriving via Malmö may take the Pågatåg (local commuter train) or the Øresundståg from Malmö Central to Lund Central. Tickets are available through the Skånetrafiken app.

Lund Central Station → Faculty of Law

On foot ~5 min

From Lund Central Station, proceed southeast along Klostergatan towards Lund Cathedral. Juridicum is situated at Lilla Gråbrödersgatan 4, a short walk from the station.

General Information

Travel Documents & Entry

Sweden is part of the Schengen Area. EU/EEA nationals may enter with a valid passport or national identity card. Participants from outside the Schengen Area should verify visa requirements with their nearest Swedish embassy or consult the Swedish Migration Agency.

Accommodation

Participants are responsible for arranging their own accommodation. Both Lund and Malmö are suitable bases for the conference. Malmö is approximately 12 minutes from Lund by train, with frequent departures throughout the day.

Conference Language

The conference will be conducted entirely in English. No interpretation services will be provided.

Internet Access

Eduroam is available throughout the Lund University campus, including Juridicum. Participants affiliated with a participating institution may connect using their home credentials. Guest WiFi will also be provided.
